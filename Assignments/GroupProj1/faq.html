Ice Cream Frequent Questions

  • What are portion sizes?
      Before any mix-ins are added, our individual portion sizes are referred to as:
    • Small - approximately 5 oz. Medium - approximately 8 oz. Large - approximately 12 oz.
    • We also offer our smooth and creamy ice cream in larger portion sizes To Go
  • What is a create your own?
    • A creation combines super-premium ice cream with a variety of mix-ins, like fruit, nuts, candies and other goodies customized for each customer, creating a mouthwatering dessert. Creations come in many forms, from waffle cones and sugar cones. You can choose from our most popular creations or you can create your own creation!
  • What is a popular creation?
    • An ice cream store creation is a favorite ice cream recipe that features a special combination of ice cream and mix-ins. Some creations are tried and true favorites with a new twist, like Chocolate Chocolate Mint; others are our own unique ice cream innovations, like Birthday Party Celebration.
